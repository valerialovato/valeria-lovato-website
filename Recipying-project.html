Your recipes all organized in one place.

Problem

If you like cooking (but also if you don’t!), you sure know the frustration of having all your recipes in different places and the desire to find a fast and easy way to collect the new ones and to share them with your family or friends. Also, sometimes you need just a box where you can choose what to cook for dinner depending on what there’s in your refrigerator.

Possible solution

Recipying is your cooking book, all catalogued and organised, from the old recipes that grandma gave you written on a paper towel to the inspirations you find daily on the internet. You can upload manually you recipes, but also through a picture and from a website.

Design Thinking Process

Competitors analysis

Overview considerations:

  • Mostly related to grocery list and weekly plan
  • Not very user friendly
  • Too laborious for the user to insert new recipe
  • Difficult to share recipes

Main functions / goals

  • Add recipes easily
  • Add recipes from pictures with text processor
  • Organize through categories
  • Search based on ingredients
  • Plan events
  • Traking of the cooking process
  • Share recipes easily
  • Find suggestions

Personas

The definition of personas, together with the analytical part, is they way the needs of the users are put in context and prioritised. I have found two personas’ profiles: Jenny and Tom. Jenny is a graphic designer passionate of cooking. Tom is father of two, married, that needs to take care of his share of householding.

Jenny

  • woman
  • 35 years old
  • living alone
  • graphic designer
  • good knlowledge of IT
  • passion for cooking
  • likes dinners with friends
  • vegetarian

Jenny is really keen on cooking and as soon as she has a moment she likes cooking for herself or organizing dinners with her friends. On a average day, Jenny works from home and, since she is very organized, she keeps a weekly planning of the meals. She likes experimentig with new recipes that she needs to write down easily and also reproduces dishes of her tradition, that she has on her pc, flying papers, texts from her mum...

Key points

  • Jenny needs a place to store/organize all of her recipes
  • Jenny needs a quick tool to write down the new recipes she creates
  • She is interested in new suggestions and inspirations
  • She’d like to consult and group the recipes for weekly meals or a meal with friends

Quotes

“ I feel very frustaded when I know I have a specific recipe somewhere and I don’t remember if it is on my pc, in my bookmarks or written on my notebook. So I spend half an hour looking for it!” “ When I’m cooking is kinda like a creative process. So I’m with my hands in the dough and I need to quikly write down the ingredients and quantities. My phone ends up covered in food!”

Tom

  • man
  • 43 years old
  • family with wife and 2 kids
  • accountant
  • reasonable knlowledge of IT
  • doesn’t know much abt cooking
  • cooks for all the family daily
  • omnivorous

Tom is a young dad that works in the city center and with little time to take care of the housekeeping tasks. He is in charge of preparing dinner every evening for Emma and the kids, but doesn’t know much about cooking. He’d like a tool for having the recipes he learnt and feel confident on all written down and in order to save searching time. Also to easily share new dishes with Emma when he needs new ideas.

Key points

  • Tom needs a place to store/organize the recipes
  • Tom needs a tool to share recipes with Emma
  • He needs to save time
  • He needs to goup weekly meals

Quotes

“When I have to cook I can’t remember anything I’ve already cook! I need a quick reminder and inspiration.” “Let say I have 2 kids, 1 onion, rice, broccoli and artichokes in the refrigerator...suggestions??”

Key learnings

  • Being organized is very important to accomplish aout task effectively. Also when cooking.
  • Considering that often writing down the process of a recipe when it’s in the making is crucial.
  • Time is really important, it’s should be easy to find inspirations and recipes for a meal.
  • The possibility to group recipes into weekly plans and meals is a good asset.
