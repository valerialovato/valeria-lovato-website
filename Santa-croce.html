✽ SANTA CROCE ✽

Santa Croce is a semi-natural lake, located in the Alpago valley and surrounded by the mountains "Prealpi" Bellunesi. It is a special place because its comformation lets strong winds to enter the valley at a certain time in the day, usually in the morning and it allows the practise of several water sports such as windsurf, kitesurf and sailing.

Apart from the obective beauty of this lake, I have a special attachment to it because I'm often there with my family and it is my happy place where I find peace of mind and tranquillity whenever I crave for it. So I wanted to picture it through my eyes, to have it with me even when I'm far away, so that I can try to find that state of mind when I need it.
